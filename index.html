Gul Asghar Khan Baghoor is a prominent Pakistani politician who has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since February 2024. In September 2024, he was appointed as Federal Parliamentary Secretary for Ministry of Communications, Government of Pakistan.

He represents the constituency NA-88 Khushab-II and is a member of the Istehkam-e-Pakistan Party (IPP). His dedication to public service and development initiatives has made him a respected figure in Pakistani politics.
